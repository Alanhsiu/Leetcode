The behavioral interview asks one thing in many forms: *show me, with a real example, how
you work.* The questions are predictable; the failure mode is rambling, vague, or
hero-not-team answers. The fix is a small library of **specific, structured stories** you
can adapt on the fly.

Start with **[The STAR method](/behavioral/star-method)** for the structure, then use
**[Common questions](/behavioral/common-questions)** to build your story bank.
